Government Schemes

To support Pregnant Women and lactating Mothers(pre/post/During Pregnancy

  • Pradhan Mantri Matritva Vandhana Yojana

  • Mother and Child Tracking System (MCTS)

    MCTS was launched in 2009. The scheme ensures that the mother and their children get the medical services and care which includes pregnancy care, delivery services, and timely immunizations.

  • The Indira Gandhi Matritva Sahyog Yojana, Conditional Maternity Benefit plan (CMB)

    The IGMSY is a central government scheme for the pregnant and lactating mothers who are 19 or above 19. The benefits under the IGMSY are provided only fort he first two live births.

  • Pan-India expansion of the Maternity Benefits Programme (MBP)

    to benefit pregnant and lactating mothers

  • Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act 2016

    Under the Act, 26 weeks maternity leave has been granted to the pregnant woman, earlier it was 12 weeks, for the first two living children.
